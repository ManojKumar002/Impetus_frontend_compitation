Welcome

MILAGRO is an annual inter-collegiate cultural fest and the biggest fest in UVCE (Bangalore University). This two-day cultural extravaganza is the most anticipated fest in UVCE and is greeted with pomp and show. It generally witnesses a footfall of over 4000 students from several colleges in and around Bangalore. With over 40 events like Fashion Show, Just Dance, Singing, Mad Ads, Drama and must to attend performances by Bands and DJ Night; it sets ablaze the campus with participants battling it out to win prizes.

MILAGRO is indeed a season of jubilant smiles, whooping cries of victory, ecstatic laughter and bonding and has always enthralled the audience and the participants alike with its magnificence.
